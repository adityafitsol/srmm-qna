---

## HOW SRMM SCORE IS DEFINED

| Level | Stage | BRSR Score (% of Grand Total) | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------------------------|-------------|
| Level 1 | Formative Stage | Up to 25% | Initial level — identifying need & responsibility of BRSR |
| Level 2 | Emerging Stage | >25% and Up to 50% | Realises BRSR value; setting up robust mechanism |
| Level 3 | Established Stage | >50% and Up to 75% | Formal functions/policies/systems established |
| Level 4 | Leading by Example | >75% | Strive for more than compliance; market leader |

**Score Structure:**
- Section A + B: 42 points (Essential only)
- Section C (9 Principles): 258 points (Essential: 183 | Leadership: 75)
- **Grand Total = 300** (Essential: 225 | Leadership: 75)

> **Note:** If any clause is not applicable to a corporate, Grand Total is reduced accordingly. Maturity % = (Score obtained / Revised Grand Total) × 100

```
Score % = (Total Score Obtained / Grand Total*) × 100
                            ↓
        ┌──────────────────────────────────────┐
        │  ≤ 25%  → Level 1: Formative Stage   │
        │  26-50% → Level 2: Emerging Stage    │
        │  51-75% → Level 3: Established Stage │
        │  > 75%  → Level 4: Leading by Example│
        └──────────────────────────────────────┘

* Grand Total = 300 minus any non-applicable clause scores
```

**Leadership Indicators (75 pts)** are given high prominence to encourage companies to target achievement beyond basic compliance — companies that answer Leadership Indicators well move rapidly toward Level 4.
